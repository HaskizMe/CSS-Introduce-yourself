Some of our country's traditions in the United States are: Thanksgiving,
4th of July, and Christmas.

4th of July is celebrated to remember the passage
of the Declaration of Independence by the
Continental Congress on July 4, 1776.

Thanksgiving is November 24th. It is a time we meet with family and have a big feast to celebrate a good "harvest."

Christmas is celebrated on Decemeber 25th. It is celebrated to remember the birth of Jesus Christ.
